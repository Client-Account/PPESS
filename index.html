GPS RTK and PPK

GPS RTK (Real-Time Kinematic) provides instant, centimeter-level positioning for real-time field surveys, while PPK (Post-Processing Kinematic) offers high-precision location data by processing GPS observations after data collection, enhancing survey accuracy in challenging environments.

Auto Level/Dumpy Level

A dumpy level, builder’s auto level, leveling instrument, or automatic level is an optical instrument used to establish or verify points in the same horizontal plane. It is used in surveying and building with a vertical staff to measure height differences and to transfer, measure and set heights.
